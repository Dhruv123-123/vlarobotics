The open standard for
physical AI safety.

PAVS is a vendor-neutral framework defining what it means for a robot policy to be deployment-ready. We provide the evaluation methodology, benchmark suite, and certification infrastructure that the physical AI industry currently lacks.

Built by practitioners who have watched capable models fail in the real world — not because the AI wasn't good enough, but because nobody had defined what "good enough" means when steel meets the physical world.

PAVS is free to implement. Certification is fee-based and operated independently of any hardware vendor or model developer.
